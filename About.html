Why I create this website

PRS is actually a common seen uniform group in many schools, but there are still many people who do not understand the mission of this group and mainly do what. Hence, the purpose of this website is to let more people know and understand its mission. In addition, this is also an opportunity to let my future junior to know more about PRS and fall in love with this group as possible.

Why I chose to become PRS

My brother and sister were part of the PRS. When I was primary school and just went to high school, I could saw them stay at the school to help with the PRS activities every day. I always feel that they have a sense of responsibility, and I want to become a responsible person like them one day. After I joined in form 2, I learned more than just a sense of responsibility, but more knowledge and skills. I am grateful that I did not choose to join the wrong group.
