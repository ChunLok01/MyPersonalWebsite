When I officially become a part of PRS.

I was applied to join PRS when I was form 2 in secondary school. Soon, I passed the interview and started an 8-month training course. During the training, the teacher taught us a lot about counseling and communication skills. I had learned about the mission and vision of PRS throughout the course. The 8-month training actually passed quickly. I also met many friends during the training, and I had a good relationship with the teacher. After the training, the seniors had given a test to us. Fortunately, I passed the test and will soon put on a purple school uniform when I was form 3 in the next year to become a part of the PRS.

One day in December of the same year, the seniors held a tie handover ceremony for us. A purple school uniform with a tie specially made by the school for us is a symbol of our PRS. On that day, my partners and I changed into the school uniforms we had bought before and came to the auditorium of the school. We are all very excited, because this is the first time we have put on the purple school uniform that we have long admired, and we are ready to take our own tie from the teacher. After the teacher's speech, the seniors began to take turns calling our name to take the tie. In the process of waiting, we all made fun of each other because we had never seen each other wearing purple school uniforms. Soon after, the senior called my name. I walked on stage with tension and excitement. In fact, I think it is more embarrassing, because everyone is watching me with a teasing look under the stage.

When everyone got the tie, the seniors began to teach us how to wear the tie properly. We all listened carefully in the under the stage and followed the instructions given by the seniors to wear the tie step by step. There are some people among us still don't understand how to wear the tie. So, the seniors patiently taught him personally. For me, although I was also still not really understand the step, I had cooperated with my friends to find out and correct each other's steps to wear the tie. In the end, everyone had learned all the steps to wear the tie smoothly.

This was a very meaningful day because I finally became a part of PRS on this day. After becoming a PRS, I want to use my abilities to help my friends around me, the teacher and also myself.
